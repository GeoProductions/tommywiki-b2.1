Tommy

The main character of the show. On the outside, he seems to be an ordinary schoolkid, but don’t be fooled. Deep inside, he’s riddled with special powers, which’ll help him smash though anything in his or his friends’ path. He has a brother by the name of Hunter.

Christopher ‘Long Potato Bauble Head’ Jones

A ninja, but has always been quite clueless as a ninja and has clearly never had any intent of being so, as clearly seen in ‘Road to Ninja’. Instead, he pursues a role in education, joining Tommy and his friends’ school (before they would have joined) fresh after leaving that exact school as a student in year 11 in 2007. He was a teaching assistant there for a few years before leaving to go back to the ninjas. After the ninja arc (in S2E3), he’d return to the school along with his best friend Assassin Boldheart. They’d teach alongside eachother for a while but would eventually to each teach their own subjects (Geography for Mr Jones and history or sometimes PE for Mr Boldheart). Despite being a ninja, he’s always been an ally of Tommy and his mates. Chris is often seen as the more ‘boring’ of the two main ninjas, having less of an interest in strength and combat and instead opting to pursue his own goals of being a teacher.

Lindsay (Initially known as ‘Big Hair Girl’)

Is depicted as one of those ‘VSCO Girls’ fresh out of 2019 TikTok. Like them, she’s seen reciting infamous lines such as ‘sksksk’ and ‘Save the turtles!’. Additionally, for reasons unknown, she also likes going around with her feet out in situations where you wouldn't usually expect someone to, with seemingly no regard for how odd she may come across as for doing so.

Emily

Another annoying girl at school. Since the Christmas hunger games special 2021, she’s been showed to be dating Karl/Chris, usually doing furry stuff together.

Karl/Chris

a guy from Tommy’s school who, as per usual, is somewhat of an annoyance. He is often seen complaining quite vocally when he doesn’t like something. Since the Christmas special 2021, he’s been depicted to be dating Emily.

Melbourne

One of Tommy’s best friends, specifically the one he met in the second series. His dad is the In S3E5, he moves in to Tommy’s care home, Green Ridge, transferring from his former home of Bob’s Farm Care Home.

Mr. Carrot

He was the internal substitute teacher for Tommy’s school, once shown to be scared of leaving his main room (Spare Classroom 7), forcing any classes he covers to come to him rather than him going to them. He is married to Miss Carrot and eventually left the role.

Miss Carrot

Another sub at school, and also does admin work such as informing people of a closure in S3E9. She is married to Mr Carrot. She was usually stationed in Spare Classroom 6 until the room was reassigned to a different purpose.

Johnny

He is the first friend Tommy meets early on in the first series. He helps Tommy through the first initial challenges he faces.

 

Billy

The second friend of Tommy, we met him in S1E5 when Tommy and Johnny were granted a day off of school and they were out on the streets.

Steve

He was actually created purely on accident. The S2 theme song incorrectly had a background line that said “Yo Tommy, I’m Steve!”, and thus he had to be written into the show. We first see him in S3E1, where he’s seen been attacked by this huge fishy thingy. It’s up to Tommy and his friends to save Steve, after which they would go on to be friends.

Assassin Boldheart

The other one of the two main ninjas. He’s also a teacher but he’s definitely a lot more into being a ninja rather than teaching, compared to Long Potato Bauble Head, who is more into teaching. He teaches History and PE, and originally co-taught with Long Potato Bauble head whilst the duo were first settling in. His surname is very rarely mentioned as he much prefers to just go by “Assassin”. The only time students even learn his last name is on their timetables.

EO/Crazy EO/Eron

The god of the ‘Tommy’ universe. He is the real person who came up with the show and writes for it. Played by himself when it comes to voice acting.

Geo/Geo Mario

He is, again, a real-life person, being the CEO of Geo Productions, which is the company responsible for publishing the show. He’s also known for breaking the 4th wall a lot, although this is being phased out. Unlike EO, he’s not depicted as any kind of god, just a weird inter-universal friend of Tommy and his circle. He’s seen to have issues with contacting them from his home world (Earth) and often had to make trips to and from the ‘Tommy’ universe. This was supposed to support the initial idea of him being a temporary character, but he ended up staying.

Micheal

A key rival of Tommy’s. In series 3, he seems to have a huge obsession with being awarded the ‘minis’ badge, originally mentioned in S1E4, to destroy Tommy’s chances of getting it himself. Also played by himself for voice acting.

Melbourne’s Dad/Bob

The father of Melbourne. To begin with, his name remained a mystery but in S3E7 his name was revealed to be Bob. In S3E2, he first shows up in the alien mother ship and he’s revealed to be the leader of the aliens. He dislikes his son. Despite his son previously living in the care home, and them sharing a name, Bob has no canon affiliation with Bob’s Farm Care Home.

Hunter

Tommy’s brother, rarely seen as of right now, but he also appears as one of the two main characters in ‘Ultimate Climbers’, although canonically speaking he’s classed as a different entity in each show. He shares a room with Tommy in Green Ridge Care Home.

In the show, various headteachers have appeared. In series 1, the headteacher is fired due to a scandal involving Tommy. During the alien bandit arc, Mr Mick holds the title. Other headteachers are also seen throughout the show with minimal backstory.

Mr. Wilder (Road To Ninja)

He was a science teacher at Tommy’s school until his anger issues caused him to crash out at his class in 2008 including him ripping an interactive display off the wall writing it off. Needless to say he was fired immediately but he reappeared in Road To Ninja.
