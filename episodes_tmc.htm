Tommy wins the ‘Minis’ badge at school and is sought after by kidnappers. Once captured using a sly plan involving a fake school bus, Tommy has to rely on his friend Johnny to help him escape.

 

This episode features an experimental live-action clip with props showing the kidnapping. The format of having Tommy take place in a different animation style was planned to be reused once per series, series 2’s example was to be ‘Tommy Minecraft’, but a technical issue meant it had to be uploaded as a spin-off. This was changed to the idea of doing a spin-off in a different video game every series, but that was ultimately scrapped too.
