27/11/2020

no u

Tommy is issued a detention, and the situation quickly escalates into multiple staff being fired.

The first episode of ‘Tommy’. Tommy is placed in detention unfairly and the teacher faces consequences, leading to the teacher retaliating and knocking out the headteacher. The episode somehow results in Tommy winning the headteacher title.

EO, Geo

On Friday 27th November 2020, Geo Productions CEO Geo Mario and ‘Tommy’ creator Crazy EO were on a call as they often do, EO brought up the in-call ‘Whiteboard’, and started illustrating a story using the various tools available. Geo saw the potential in this and production started that same night.

17/12/2020

KIDNAPPED

Tommy gets kidnapped after getting a badge at school, can his new friend save him?

Tommy wins the ‘Minis’ badge at school and is sought after by kidnappers. Once captured using a sly plan involving a fake school bus, Tommy has to rely on his friend Johnny to help him escape.

EO, Geo

This episode features an experimental live-animated clip with props showing the kidnapping. The format of having Tommy take place in a different animation style was planned to be reused once per series, series 2’s example was to be ‘Tommy Minecraft’, but a technical issue meant it had to be uploaded as a spin-off. This was changed to the idea of doing a spin-off in a different video game every series, but that was ultimately scrapped too.

During production for the original edit of this episode, vital editing assets for the show couldn’t be accessed. This resulted in the intro being reproduced for the recording.
